History

6 years ago I really started to notice the ammount of old cans and plastics bottles that I was simply throwing away to be put in a landfill somewhere. I recongnized the unneeded waste that I was adding to the envirnoment and decided I was going to try and make a small difference. So I collected some old cans and plastic bottles and used them to create an lawn chair. I really enjoyed creating the chair, and found that other people admired my work. So I started making more cahirs and selling them to friends and family. I was saving so many items that would otherwise just be thrown away that I make the decision to turn it into a business. Today our business takes donations of old cans and bottles and turns them into furniture for sale.

Why Us?

Stop by our shop with all of your old cans and bottles we will gladly take them and turn them into furniture. While you are there please look around to see if there is anything for you, all of our furniture is 100% made of recycled materials. When you buy our furniture you're adding style and comfort to your home while also doing your part in helping our planet.
